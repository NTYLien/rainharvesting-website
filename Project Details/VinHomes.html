VinHomes Central Park

Vinhomes Central Park is Vingroup's first major project in Ho Chi Minh City, inspired by the world-famous Central Park New York area. Possessing rare golden coordinates, Vinhomes Central Park location at 208 Nguyen Huu Canh, Ward 22, Binh Thanh District is considered the economic center of the city.
